Kori Trek

Overview

The Kori Trek offers a unique experience for adventurers looking to explore the less-visited, untouched beauty of Nepal's remote areas. This trek takes you through lush forests, ancient villages, and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. It is a perfect mix of nature and culture.

Itinerary

Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights

  • Day 1: Kathmandu to Pokhara - Drive to Pokhara and enjoy a beautiful lakeside evening.
  • Day 2: Pokhara to Sikles - Begin the trek with a scenic hike to Kori Village.
  • Day 3: Sikles to Hugu Goth - Ascend through forests and enjoy panoramic mountain views.
  • Day 4: Hugu Goth to Kapuche Lake - Early morning hike to Kapuche Glacier Lake (approx. 3 hours round trip). Return to Hugu Goth for accommodation.
  • Day 5: Hugu Goth to Nontha - Trek through deciduous forests and suspension bridges.
  • Day 6: Nontha to Kori Danda - Early morning trek to Kori Danda for panoramic mountain views.
  • Day 7: Kori Danda to Sikles - Descend back to Sikles village. Overnight at a homestay.
  • Day 8: Sikles to Pokhara - Drive back to Pokhara.

Important Details:

  • Days: 8
  • Difficulty: 4 of 5 (Strenuous)
  • Min Group Size: 1
  • Max Group Size: 14
  • Trek Region: Annapurna Region
  • Type of Trek: Private Trip
  • Highest Elevation: 3,500 m (11,483 ft)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-May) & Autumn (Sept-Nov)
  • Transportation: Private Vehicle
