Everest Base Camp Trek

The Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the most iconic treks in the world, offering incredible views of Mount Everest, the highest peak on Earth. This trek allows you to experience Sherpa culture, stunning landscapes, and breathtaking mountain views.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu. On arrival, you will be received by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Rest and refresh.
  • Day 2: Kathmandu to Lukla and Trek to Phakding. We take a scenic flight to Lukla (2800m) and begin our trek to Phakding.
  • Day 3: Phakding to Namche Bazaar. Today, we cross suspension bridges, trek through pine forests, and reach the bustling market town of Namche Bazaar.
  • Day 4: Acclimatization Day at Namche Bazaar. This is an important acclimatization day. We will explore the town and enjoy panoramic views of Everest and Lhotse.
  • Day 5: Namche Bazaar to Tengboche. Todayâ€™s trek takes us through forests and picturesque villages. We reach Tengboche Monastery with views of Everest and Ama Dablam.
  • Day 6: Tengboche to Dingboche. We head towards Dingboche, passing through bridges and forests while admiring majestic peaks.
  • Day 7: Acclimatization Day at Dingboche. We spend the day acclimatizing by taking a short hike to nearby ridges to get closer views of Everest, Makalu, and Lhotse.
  • Day 8: Dingboche to Lobuche. We pass through the alpine landscape and continue our journey towards Lobuche.
  • Day 9: Lobuche to Gorak Shep. Trek to Gorak Shep, where we rest and prepare for our ascent to Everest Base Camp the next day.
  • Day 10: Gorak Shep to Everest Base Camp. This is the highlight of the trek as we reach Everest Base Camp (5364m) and enjoy an up-close view of the highest mountain in the world.
  • Day 11: Gorak Shep to Kala Patthar and descend to Pheriche. We wake up early to hike up to Kala Patthar (5550m) for the most iconic view of Mount Everest.
  • Day 12: Pheriche to Namche Bazaar. We begin our descent, retracing our steps back through beautiful forests and villages.
  • Day 13: Namche Bazaar to Lukla. We complete the trek back to Lukla and rest after our adventure.
  • Day 14: Lukla to Kathmandu. Fly back to Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel for a farewell dinner.
  • Day 15: Departure. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home, marking the end of your incredible adventure.

Important Details:

  • Days: 15
  • Difficulty: 4 of 5 (Strenuous)
  • Min Group Size: 1
  • Max Group Size: 14
  • Trek Region: Everest Region
  • Type of Trek: Private Trip
  • Highest Elevation: 5,550 m (18,209 ft)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-April-May) & Autumn (Sep-Oct-Nov) & Winter (Dec-Jan-Feb)
  • Transportation: Domestic Flight and Private Vehicle
