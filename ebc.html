Everest Base Camp Trek

The Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the most iconic treks in the world, offering incredible views of Mount Everest, the highest peak on Earth. This trek allows you to experience Sherpa culture, stunning landscapes, and breathtaking mountain views.

Itineary

Day 1: Kathmandu to Lukla – Phakding

Fly from Kathmandu to Lukla (2,860 m), then trek to Phakding (2,610 m) — approx. 3-4 hours.

Day 2: Phakding to Namche Bazaar

Trek through pine forests and suspension bridges to reach Namche Bazaar (3,440 m) — approx. 6-7 hours.

Day 3: Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

Explore Namche Bazaar or hike to Everest View Hotel for acclimatization and panoramic mountain views.

Day 4: Namche Bazaar to Tengboche

Trek to Tengboche (3,867 m), home to the famous Tengboche Monastery — approx. 5-6 hours.

Day 5: Tengboche to Dingboche

Pass through forests and rivers as you head to Dingboche (4,410 m) — approx. 5-6 hours.

Day 6: Acclimatization Day in Dingboche

Optional hike to Nagarjun Hill for altitude adjustment and breathtaking views.

Day 7: Dingboche to Lobuche

Trek past the Thukla Pass memorial site to Lobuche (4,940 m) — approx. 5-6 hours.

Day 8: Lobuche to Gorakshep – Everest Base Camp – Gorakshep

Trek to Gorakshep (5,170 m), then continue to Everest Base Camp (5,364 m). Return to Gorakshep for overnight stay — approx. 7-8 hours total.

Day 9: Gorakshep to Kala Patthar – Pheriche

Early morning hike to Kala Patthar (5,550 m) for sunrise views of Everest, then descend to Pheriche (4,240 m) — approx. 6-7 hours.

Day 10: Pheriche to Namche Bazaar

Retrace your steps through Tengboche back to Namche Bazaar — approx. 6-7 hours.

Day 11: Namche Bazaar to Lukla

Descend to Lukla through forests and villages — approx. 6-7 hours.

Day 12: Fly from Lukla to Kathmandu

Take a scenic morning flight back to Kathmandu. End of trek.

Important Details:

  • Days: 15
  • Difficulty: 4 of 5 (Strenuous)
  • Min Group Size: 1
  • Max Group Size: 14
  • Trek Region: Everest Region
  • Type of Trek: Private Trip
  • Highest Elevation: 5,550 m (18,209 ft)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-April-May) & Autumn (Sep-Oct-Nov) & Winter (Dec-Jan-Feb)
  • Transportation: Domestic Flight and Private Vehicle
