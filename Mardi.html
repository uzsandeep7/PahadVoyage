Mardi Himal Trek

A Scenic Trail Through Lush Forests and High Ridges

The Mardi Himal Trek offers a fantastic off-the-beaten-path adventure with stunning views of the Annapurna range, Mardi Himal, Machapuchare, and the surrounding valleys.

Itinerary

Day 1: Pokhara to Phedi - Pothana

Drive from Pokhara to Phedi (30 minutes) and trek to Pothana (2-3 hours). Enjoy the panoramic views of the Annapurna range.

Day 2: Pothana to Forest Camp

Trek through lush rhododendron forests to reach Forest Camp (6-7 hours), a beautiful place surrounded by dense forest.

Day 3: Forest Camp to Mardi Himal Base Camp

Trek to Mardi Himal Base Camp (5-6 hours). Enjoy the magnificent views of Mardi Himal, Machapuchare, and the surrounding peaks.

Day 4: Mardi Himal Base Camp to Siding Village

Descend to Siding Village (5-6 hours), a picturesque village offering great views of the surrounding hills.

Day 5: Siding Village to Pokhara

Trek back to Phedi and drive back to Pokhara (5-6 hours).

Important Details:

  • Days: 5
  • Difficulty: 3 of 5 (Moderate)
  • Min Group Size: 1
  • Max Group Size: 14
  • Trek Region: Annapurna Region
  • Type of Trek: Private Trip
  • Highest Elevation: 4,500 m (14,764 ft)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-May) & Autumn (Sept-Nov)
  • Transportation: Private Vehicle
