About Pahad Voyage Nepal

Pahad Voyage Nepal has been offering exceptional trekking experiences in Nepal for over 15 years. Our team of experienced guides and trekkers have explored the highest peaks and the most scenic routes across Nepal, providing guests with the safest, most rewarding adventures.

Our Unique Offerings

We provide essential medical facilities with trained personnel for safety and quick emergency support. Trekkers also enjoy professional photography and videography services, capturing stunning moments and creating lasting memories.

Company Values & Mission

At Pahad Voyage Nepal, we are deeply committed to sustainable tourism practices. We aim to offer eco-friendly trekking solutions that protect the environment while supporting local communities. Our goal is to ensure that every trek not only leaves lasting memories but also leaves a positive impact on the places we visit.
