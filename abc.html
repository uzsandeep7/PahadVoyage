Annapurna Base Camp Trek

The Annapurna Base Camp Trek offers a perfect combination of natural beauty and cultural diversity. Youâ€™ll trek through beautiful terraced fields, charming villages, and dense forests while enjoying magnificent views of the Annapurna range.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Arrive in Kathmandu, and transfer to your hotel.

Day 2: Kathmandu to Pokhara

Drive or fly to Pokhara.

Day 3: Pokhara to Nayapul, then trek to Tikhedhunga

Drive from Pokhara to Nayapul, then trek to Tikhedhunga.

Day 4: Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani

Trek from Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani.

Day 5: Ghorepani to Poon Hill (sunrise view) and then trek to Tadapani

Early morning hike to Poon Hill for a stunning sunrise view, then continue trekking to Tadapani.

Day 6: Tadapani to Sinuwa

Trek from Tadapani to Sinuwa.

Day 7: Sinuwa to Deurali

Trek from Sinuwa to Deurali.

Day 8: Deurali to Annapurna Base Camp (ABC)

Reach the base camp and enjoy panoramic mountain views.

Day 9: ABC to Bamboo

Trek from ABC to Bamboo.

Day 10: Bamboo to Nayapul, then drive back to Pokhara

Trek from Bamboo to Nayapul, then drive back to Pokhara.

Day 11: Drive back to Kathmandu

Drive back to Kathmandu.

Important Details:

  • Days: 11
  • Difficulty: 4 of 5 (Strenuous)
  • Min Group Size: 1
  • Max Group Size: 14
  • Trek Region: Annapurna Region
  • Type of Trek: Private Trip
  • Highest Elevation: 4,130 m (13,549 ft)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-May) & Autumn (Sept-Nov)
  • Transportation: Private Vehicle
