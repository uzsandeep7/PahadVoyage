Ghorepani: Poon Hill

The Best Sunrise Viewpoint in Nepal

This trek takes you to Ghorepani, a beautiful village offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The highlight is the Poon Hill viewpoint, which offers an incredible sunrise view of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges.

Itinerary

Day 1: Pokhara to Nayapul - Tikhedhunga

Drive from Pokhara to Nayapul (1.5 hours), then trek to Tikhedhunga (approximately 3-4 hours).

Day 2: Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani

Trek from Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani (5-6 hours). Enjoy scenic views of the surrounding hills and mountains.

Day 3: Ghorepani to Poon Hill - Tadapani

Early morning hike to Poon Hill for a stunning sunrise view, then continue trekking to Tadapani (6-7 hours).

Day 4: Tadapani to Ghandruk

Descend to Ghandruk, a beautiful Gurung village (3-4 hours).

Day 5: Ghandruk to Nayapul - Pokhara

Trek back to Nayapul, then drive back to Pokhara (5-6 hours).
